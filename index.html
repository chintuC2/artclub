About US

Art Club GCOEA is a college level club for all art enthusiasts who want to explore, develop and utilize their artistic skills and creativity to showcase it to society. It was formed on 11 january 2014. It conducts the sessions on every weekend to teach various art forms and techniques, this sessions are either senior art club members or the expert faculties from other art institutes and classes. Art Club conducts mini exhibition (Art Gallery) in Alumini Meet of college every year and another exhibition (Art Adroit) during Prajwalan (Technical festival of college), apart from developing art skills as a hobby or interest, it also guide's student's who want to pursue art and design education and make career in it. It organizes guidance seminars about design education, preparation of exams like NID, CEED which are conducted by passed out Art Club members and other experts and alumini of NID , IIIT ,IIT IDC. 

  • 2014-2015

    Our Humble Beginnings

    The Art Club was formed on 11 January 2014. It was the only club formed totally with students support. We approached interested faculties and managed to form a base for this platform, and now this club hass become an independent body which is handled by our fellow mates.

  • 2015-2016

    And The Legacy Began

    Being first club to start in oddly manner was difficult but still the group never gave up. Rather they evolved and carried each and every aspect carefully. The club which merely had members began to grow huge in no time.

  • 2016-2017

    New ERA 

    The Art Club initially started as a group of some people keenly interested in art now had over hundreds of students who joined. Every member had something special about themselves. The more the count increased so does the art forms we began to see.

  • 2017-2018

    Evolution

    Its just been three years and our club had become the only club to carry this far . All this was possible because of the members who never gave up on the club. With new abilities each one evolved and took the club to new level. We even started the digital art. 

  • 2018-2019

    Transition to Full Service

    As time passed by the Club expanded more . The students from club appeared for university level competetions and stood colour coat holder.

  • 2019-2020

    The Legacy Continues

    Its been six years since this family Art Club came into existance, and till day its one of the best club this college ever had. The art we inherited and evolved will be passed down to upcomings.
